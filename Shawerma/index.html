Here's Exactly How To Make The Best Shawerma at home

So it's midnight and you're craving sandwiches but don't want to order in for the fivth time this week? we've got your back, go get chicken, some spices, lemon and garlic ready and you'll be having the best SHAWERMA of your life in no time.

MAIN INGREDIANTS

Ingredient Amount
chicken thigh fillets 800 Gr
Lemon juice 1 tbsp
Olive oil 4 tbsp
Spices- coriander, cumin, cardamon 1 tbsp each
ground cayenne pepper 1 tsp
garlic 1 large clove
Arabic or Peta bread 4-5 Loafs
Sliced lettuce, onions and tomatos to taste

SAUCE OPTIONS FOR SHAWARMA

You can use chili sauce, tahini sauce, garlic sause or even better you can use all of them.

While Tahini sauce is just watered down Tahini, garlic sauce is the following ingrediants mixed up together:

  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • yoghurt
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cumin

For better results be generous on the garlic!

Barely enough!

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix marinade – For convenience, just mix the marinade directly in a ziplock bag.
    A bag works best because the chicken remains nicely coated in the marinade.

  2. Add chicken – into the marinade. Seal the bag, removing excess air, then massage to coat from the outside. Leave to marinade.
  3. Garlic sauce – simply by mix the ingredients then set aside for at least 20 minutes to let the flavours meld.
  4. Cook chicken – either on the stove or on the BBQ. It will get a great crust on it from the spices, and you will adore the smell. It's intoxicating!
  5. Rest chicken – for at least 3 minutes before serving to allow the juices to redistribute throughout the flesh, else they will just run out everywhere when you slice the meat.
  6. To serve – just pile everything on a platter and let everybody make their own wraps!
